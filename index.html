Luxurious villa Astrea in Ithaca

Villa Astrea is located in Perachori, a quiet and cozy village, 3 km south of Vathy, Ithaca's capital town.

Ithaka, arguably the most famous island.

Ionian sea, clean and tropical, here you will find some of the finest beaches with turquise waters. Gidaki, afales, marmagas, mprosta aetos, sxoinos, ai-giannis and the list goes on.

Cozy, beautyful, peaceful.


Beaches of pebble and stone are couched in deeply carved bays and much of the Ithaka countryside is heartbreakingly beautiful.
